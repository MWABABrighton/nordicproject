Who We Are

Welcome to our vibrant non-profit association dedicated to promoting Zambian culture in the Nordic and Baltic regions. We are committed to creating an inclusive community where Zambians can actively participate and share their ideas and talents.

Our goal is to build strong connections within our community and with Zambia, facilitating mutual support and collaboration for a brighter future.

Join us in our mission to embrace and share the beauty of Zambian culture!
