Our Mission, Purpose, and Vision

Mission

To promote and work together with businesses, organizations, and communities in investment, development, and education in the Nordic region and Zambia.

Purpose

To create awareness about Zambian culture, foster unity, and ease integration for Zambians in the Nordic/Baltic region through events, workshops, and exchange programs.

Vision

To build strong partnerships and collaborative opportunities that benefit both Zambia and the Nordic/Baltic regions, promoting cultural and economic growth.
